Welcome To ShareNess


"Hunger is not a problem. It is an obscenity.
How wonderful it is that nobody need wait a single
moment before starting to improve the world".– Anne Frank

ShareNess is an organisation
which aims to redistribute excess food
from weddings, parties, events
to those who are hungry.


The idea took root in Jamshedpur, where we started with 2 Shopper Bags and a volunteer to collect the surplus food and deliver to the homeless through Public Transportation System from August 17th, 2021. We took an oath on that day to stop food waste and satisfy the hunger of many, in a quest to make the “World Hunger Free.”
